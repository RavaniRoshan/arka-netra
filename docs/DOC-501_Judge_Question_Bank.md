# DOC-501: Judge Question Bank

## Why is ArkaNetra not just another flare classifier?

ArkaNetra outputs risk, confidence, explanation, anomaly index, and mission context. The system is framed as operational decision support rather than a single black-box probability.

## Why combine soft and hard X-rays?

Soft X-rays describe hot thermal plasma response, while hard X-rays carry information about impulsive non-thermal energy release. Their interaction is the central Aditya-L1-inspired advantage.

## Why use the Neupert effect?

The Neupert relationship links hard X-ray behavior with the derivative of soft X-ray emission. The MVP uses it as a soft physics-informed signal, not as a rigid rule.

## Is the SEP panel a validated SEP forecast?

No. It is future-facing mission context. A validated SEP model requires particle data and separate evaluation.
